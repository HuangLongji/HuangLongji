About

    Dr. Sijie Ruan is an assistant professor at School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology.

    His research interests include spatio-temporal data mining, volunteered geographic information and urban computing.

    He received B.E. and Ph.D. degrees from Xidian University in 2017 and 2022, advised by IEEE Fellow Prof. Yu Zheng.

    He has published some papers in refereed journals and conferences, such as TKDE, KDD, AAAI, ICDE, WWW and UbiComp.

    He also serves as a reviewer/PC member for several referred journals and conferences, e.g., TIST, AAAI, CIKM and MDM.

    Openings: We have openings of 3~4 master students annually. Please contact me via email if you have interest.
