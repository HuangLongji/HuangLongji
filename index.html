About

    Longji Huang is an PHD student at School of Computer Science and Technology, Xidian University.

    His research interests include spatio-temporal data mining, volunteered geographic information and urban computing.

    He received Ph.D. degrees from Xidian University in 2024, advised by IEEE Fellow Prof. Yu Zheng.

    He has published some papers in refereed journals and conferences, such as SigSpatial, KBS, Applied Intelligence.

    He also serves as a reviewer/PC member for several referred journals and conferences.
