About

    Longji Huang is an PHD student at School of Computer Science and Technology, Xidian University.

    His research interests include spatio-temporal data mining, volunteered geographic information and urban computing.

    He received Ph.D. degrees from Xidian University in 2024.

    He has published some papers in refereed journals and conferences, such as TKDD, SigSpatial, KBS, Applied Intelligence.

    He also serves as a reviewer for several referred journals and conferences.
